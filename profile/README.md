## 🎯 使命 / Our Mission

> **让每一个人都能借助 AI 开启自己的一人公司。**
>
> **Empower every individual to build their own AI-driven One-Person Company.**

OPC Space 是一个围绕 **AI 一人公司（One-Person Company, OPC）** 建立的开放创业社区。我们不卖课、不撮合、不做平台抽成，只连接**有相同理念的个体创业者**，共享政策信息、产业洞察、技术工具与落地经验。

OPC Space is an open community for AI-powered One-Person Companies. We don't sell courses, broker deals, or take commissions — we connect **solo entrepreneurs with shared values** to exchange policy insights, industry research, tools, and hands-on experience.
